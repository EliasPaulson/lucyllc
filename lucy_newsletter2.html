Launched in the fall of 2016, the intention of LUCY has been to establish a collective for music and sound—working to expand sonic aesthetic within the worlds of fashion, art, beauty and film. Since it’s inception, LUCY has cultivated a network of musicians and composers to craft landscapes of sound for our clients. As we prepare for upcoming summer projects, below are some of the highlights of the last six months.

SAY HELLO
Alongside director and photographer Pamela Hanson, LUCY composed the music for the Lingua Franca Resistance series video, which highlighted the cashmere brand’s politically charged messages on real women. The 2016 Chloé campaign featured an ethereal composition, contributing to the overall mood of the film.
